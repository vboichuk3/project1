Why Learn Anchor?

As learning & development managers, you are responsible for ensuring that workers attend all their compliance and competency training. Missing any could result in lost work hours, failed audits or even penalties for your company. Spreadsheets no longer cut it because of your growing workforce. You need a better solution.

Learn Anchor automates the training management processes to free you from this administrative burden. It helps you better organize your training records, and empowers everyone to take ownership over their learning.
